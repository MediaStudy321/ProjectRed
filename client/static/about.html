Welcome to Hell's Colosseum!

Rules

Welcome to Hell's Coliseum! Hell's Coliseum is a multiplayer, fantasy RPG with wacky demons, intense battles, and engaging game-play. The player is thrusted headfirst into the depths of the underworld and embarks on a quest to escape the firey wasteland! To begin, the player is registered as a champion to fight in the coliseum! The player is provided starter characters for their first battles. After completing the tutorial, the player may proceed to the first level of the coliseum, the Lake of Fire! The player must battle their way through each level until they face the ultimate champion. Each successful battle will grant the player gold in which they can use to trade characters, sell characters, and buy loot boxes which allow them to roll for a chance to win new and exciting characters! Battles will become increasingly difficult. The max roster size is three, and the player has opportunities to trade and sell these characters with other players. Players may chat, trade, and interact with other players outside of the coliseum to learn the ropes or discuss different strategies! The provides players with the ability to participate in the community with their own discussions and battle methods! The knowlege you gain throughout these battles is essential to become the ultimate warrior, who can defeat any demon that stands in their way! Good luck!

Combat

Battles are turn-based and initiated by the player. In combat, the player may select from several actions. Each action or move has it's own purpose, whether it be inflicting damage upon the opponent or regenerating health. Each character has their own health bar and the player must conserve their health throughout the battle in order to emerge victorious. Some moves will deal more damage than others, so it is important to choose wisely. If one of the player's characters is defeated in battle, they still have additional oppurtunities with what characters remain in their roster. If all of the player's fighters are defeated, the battle is lost.

Rolls

When battles are won, the player in granted gold in which they can use to trade characters, sell characters, and purchase loot boxes which enable them to roll for a chance to win new and exciting characters! Each loot box could contain a number of different items, from characters to healing potions. There are two types of loot boxes, one with a greater chance of reciving tougher characters with less items than the other. These items and characters can be traded between players.
